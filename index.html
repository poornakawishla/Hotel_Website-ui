About LotusCrown



Hotel LotusCrown is a 5-star haven in the heart of Sri Lanka, seamlessly blending opulence with the rich cultural tapestry of the island. Immerse yourself in luxury with our Presidential and Premium Ocean View Suites. Indulge in the best of Sri Lankan and international cuisine at Restaurant Aria, or unwind at our spa and infinity pool. Whether for weddings, business meetings, or leisure, LotusCrown offers an unforgettable experience in a breathtaking setting. Welcome to a world where every moment is designed to be truly exceptional.
